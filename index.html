One in five people in the Philippines lives in extreme poverty.
Overview
As of 2023, it is reported that 22.4% of the Philippines' population are living in conditions under the poverty line.
We want to do what we can in helping reduce the number of families who are living in unsustainable conditions, which is why we want to focus on identifying factors that negatively affect households so that they may be addressed.
Problem The continuous struggle of Filipino households in sustaining their daily lives due to the prevalence of poverty in the Philippines.
Solution Utilization of data science in identifying the factors that negatively affect the finances of Filipino households in hopes of inspiring government or community-driven actions.
