Poverty is no joke.

This project aims to figure out what factors negatively affect a household's net income. Through Data Science, we hope to take a deep look and analyze the expenses and wages of different household and connect the dots on how they affect poverty in the Philippines.

Data Collection

We accessed the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) archive to gain access to data regarding the salary and wages of different households and where their expenses go.
