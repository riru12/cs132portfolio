Poverty is no joke.

United Nation'sSustainable Development Goal #1
This project aims to figure out what factors negatively affect a household's net income. By analyzing these factors, we hope to understand their relationship to poverty and inform strategies to reduce poverty rates in the Philippines to meet the target goal of reducing poverty to single-digit levels by 2028.

Overview

As of 2023, it is reported that 22.4% of the Philippines' population are living in conditions under the poverty line. A subset of this group are families that do not even have enough income to sustain themselves for their basic food needs. In concrete numbers, that's 9.79 million Filipinos who struggle to feed themselves. [1]

We want to do what we can in helping reduce the number of families who are living in unsustainable conditions, which is why we focused on analyzing the factors that may affect negative income in Filipino households.
